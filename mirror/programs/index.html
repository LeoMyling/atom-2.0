Services

Project
alignment

Clear Direction For Complex Homes

Nomada Toast brings calm structure to layered residential work, defining priorities, budgets, rooms, and decision paths before the project gathers speed.

Clear Studio Process

Jo Mendes keeps each design stage legible, from initial atmosphere studies to final room-by-room resolution.

Architectural Guidance

Receive precise guidance on proportion, materials, furnishings, and the way each room should be experienced.

Tailored Direction

Every residence is approached on its own terms, with decisions shaped around architecture, lifestyle, and atmosphere.

Residence
refinement

Refining The Way A Home Lives

At Nomada Toast, our Residence Refinement service provides clients with strategic tools and insights to boost the atmosphere, clarity, and daily experience of their residences.

Design-Led Decisions

Use a clear design direction to make confident decisions about rooms, finishes, furnishings, art, and lighting.

Tailored Spatial Strategies

We shape each strategy around the life of the home, whether the work is a single room, full residence, or architectural refresh.

Ongoing Studio Care

The studio keeps the work composed as details evolve, preserving the original design intent through delivery.
